Quality Engineer

Overview

Quality Engineers at TTech are responsible for ensuring the quality and reliability of our software systems. They work closely with development teams to implement testing strategies, automate testing processes, and maintain high-quality standards.

Career Levels

Level Scope Impact Key Responsibilities

IC1

Individual test cases and features

Team-level impact

  • Learning testing methodologies

  • Writing test cases

  • Working under guidance

  • Contributing to team success

IC2

Test automation and strategy

Cross-team impact

  • Working independently

  • Implementing test automation

  • Mentoring junior QEs

  • Leading quality initiatives

IC3

Quality strategy and architecture

Organization-wide impact

  • Quality leadership

  • Test architecture design

  • Mentoring senior QEs

  • Driving quality standards

IC1 Quality Engineer

IC1 Quality Engineer is the entry-level position for quality engineers. They focus on learning testing methodologies and contributing to team quality efforts.

IC2 Quality Engineer

IC2 Quality Engineer is the mid-level position for quality engineers. They work independently on test automation and quality initiatives.

IC3 Quality Engineer

IC3 Quality Engineer is the senior-level position for quality engineers. They are quality leaders who drive testing strategy and architecture.
