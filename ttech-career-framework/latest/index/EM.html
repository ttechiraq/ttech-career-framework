Engineering Manager

Overview

Engineering Managers at TTech are responsible for leading and developing high-performing engineering teams. They balance technical leadership with people management to drive team success and deliver business value.

Department

Department impact

  • Developing managers

  • Setting technical direction

  • Driving business success

M3 Engineering Manager

M3 Engineering Manager is the entry-level management position. They focus on leading a single team and developing team members.

M4 Engineering Manager

M4 Engineering Manager is the mid-level management position. They lead multiple teams and develop team leads.

M5 Department Engineering Manager

M5 Department Engineering Manager is the senior-level management position. They lead an engineering department and drive business success.
