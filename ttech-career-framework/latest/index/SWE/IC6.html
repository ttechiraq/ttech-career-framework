IC6 Software Engineer

Overview

IC6 Software Engineers are principal-level technical leaders who shape company-wide technical direction and influence technical standards across the company. They have significant impact across the entire company and are responsible for technical vision development and strategic technical direction.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Leadership

  • Shape company-wide technical direction

  • Drive strategic technical initiatives

  • Make company-wide architecture decisions

  • Mentor company technical leaders

  • Define technical vision

System Design

  • Design company-wide systems

  • Create technical standards and best practices

  • Drive technical strategy

  • Address company-wide technical challenges

  • Ensure system reliability and performance

Collaboration

  • Work effectively across the company

  • Build strong relationships with stakeholders

  • Drive technical alignment

  • Facilitate technical discussions

  • Share knowledge and best practices

Mentorship

  • Mentor company technical leaders

  • Guide architectural decisions

  • Share technical expertise

  • Develop technical talent

  • Build technical leadership capabilities

Expectations

Technical Excellence

  • Expert knowledge across technical areas

  • Mastery of system design

  • Ability to make company-wide technical decisions

  • Understanding of company-wide technical challenges

  • Comprehensive knowledge of industry best practices

Leadership

  • Ability to lead company-wide initiatives

  • Strong strategic decision-making skills

  • Effective communication across the company

  • Strategic thinking and planning

  • Mentorship and coaching abilities

Impact

  • Drive technical excellence across the company

  • Improve system architecture and design

  • Enhance company productivity and effectiveness

  • Foster technical innovation

  • Build technical capabilities

Career Progression

Path to IC7

  • Demonstrate senior company-wide technical leadership

  • Drive strategic technical initiatives

  • Show significant impact across the company

  • Mentor and develop technical leaders

  • Contribute to company success

Path to Management

  • Develop strong leadership skills

  • Build company-wide relationships

  • Drive technical strategy

  • Mentor and develop others

  • Show potential for people management
