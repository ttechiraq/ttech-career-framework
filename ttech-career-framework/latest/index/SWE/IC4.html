IC4 Software Engineer

Overview

IC4 Software Engineers are staff-level technical leaders who drive cross-team technical initiatives and architectural decisions. They have a significant impact across multiple teams and are responsible for complex system architecture and strategic technical decisions.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Leadership

  • Lead cross-team technical initiatives

  • Drive architectural decisions across multiple teams

  • Design and implement complex system architectures

  • Make strategic technical decisions

  • Mentor technical leaders across teams

System Design

  • Design scalable and maintainable systems

  • Create technical standards and best practices

  • Drive technical strategy across teams

  • Identify and address technical debt

  • Ensure system reliability and performance

Collaboration

  • Work effectively across multiple teams

  • Build strong relationships with stakeholders

  • Drive technical alignment between teams

  • Facilitate technical discussions and decisions

  • Share knowledge and best practices

Mentorship

  • Mentor technical leaders across teams

  • Guide architectural decisions

  • Share technical expertise

  • Develop technical talent

  • Build technical leadership capabilities

Expectations

Technical Excellence

  • Deep expertise in multiple technical areas

  • Strong system design skills

  • Ability to make complex technical decisions

  • Understanding of cross-team technical challenges

  • Knowledge of industry best practices

Leadership

  • Ability to lead cross-team initiatives

  • Strong technical decision-making skills

  • Effective communication across teams

  • Strategic thinking and planning

  • Mentorship and coaching abilities

Impact

  • Drive technical excellence across teams

  • Improve system architecture and design

  • Enhance team productivity and effectiveness

  • Foster technical innovation

  • Build technical capabilities

Career Progression

Path to IC5

  • Demonstrate department-wide technical leadership

  • Drive strategic technical initiatives

  • Show significant impact across the department

  • Mentor and develop technical leaders

  • Contribute to department success

Path to Management

  • Develop strong leadership skills

  • Build cross-team relationships

  • Drive technical strategy

  • Mentor and develop others

  • Show potential for people management
