M5 Department Engineering Manager

Overview

M5 Department Engineering Managers are senior managers who lead departments and develop department leaders. They are responsible for department success, technical excellence, and career growth across their department.

Key Responsibilities

Leadership

  • Lead departments

  • Set department goals

  • Manage department performance

  • Ensure department success

  • Build strong departments

Technical Leadership

  • Guide technical decisions

  • Ensure technical excellence

  • Manage technical debt

  • Drive best practices

  • Set technical standards

People Management

  • Develop department leaders

  • Build leadership capabilities

  • Foster department culture

  • Drive department effectiveness

  • Ensure department success

Strategy

  • Set department direction

  • Drive department initiatives

  • Align with business goals

  • Foster innovation

  • Ensure department success
