IC2 Quality Engineer

Overview

IC2 Quality Engineers at TTech lead the quality assurance efforts within their team. They design and implement comprehensive testing strategies, drive test automation initiatives, and mentor other quality engineers.

Level Expectations

Scope

  • Leads test strategy and implementation

  • Designs test automation frameworks

  • Drives quality initiatives

  • Owns quality metrics and reporting

  • Influences product quality standards

Collaborative Reach

  • Works within their team

  • Collaborates with team leadership

  • Mentors quality engineers

  • Influences testing practices within team

  • Works with product and engineering teams

Levers for Impact

  • Test strategy and architecture

  • Quality metrics and standards

  • Test automation frameworks

  • Quality process improvement

  • Team productivity and effectiveness

Core Responsibilities

Results

  • Takes full ownership of test strategy implementation and its success

  • Maintains and improves test automation with clear ownership of quality

  • Proactively drives quality improvements across teams

  • Takes responsibility for setting and enforcing quality standards

  • Delivers and maintains reliable test infrastructure with clear documentation

  • Ensures long-term sustainability of testing solutions

  • Takes ownership of quality metrics and their accuracy

  • Proactively identifies and addresses quality risks

Direction

  • Takes ownership of testing strategy and its implementation

  • Designs and maintains test architectures with long-term vision

  • Drives quality initiatives with clear ownership of outcomes

  • Takes responsibility for adoption and success of best practices

  • Proactively identifies and addresses quality improvement areas

  • Ensures proper documentation and knowledge transfer

  • Takes initiative in improving testing processes and tools

  • Maintains clear communication about quality direction and decisions

Talent

  • Conducts technical interviews

  • Provides technical guidance

  • Shares knowledge across teams

  • Develops testing expertise

Culture

  • Promotes testing best practices

  • Advocates for quality standards

  • Leads by example

  • Encourages continuous improvement
