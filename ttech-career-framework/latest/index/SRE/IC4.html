IC4 Site Reliability Engineer

Overview

IC4 Site Reliability Engineers are staff-level technical leaders who drive cross-team reliability initiatives and reliability decisions. They have a significant impact across multiple teams and are responsible for complex reliability strategy and strategic reliability decisions.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Leadership

  • Lead cross-team reliability initiatives

  • Drive reliability decisions across multiple teams

  • Design and implement complex reliability strategies

  • Make strategic reliability decisions

  • Mentor reliability leaders across teams

System Design

  • Design scalable and maintainable reliability systems

  • Create reliability standards and best practices

  • Drive reliability strategy across teams

  • Identify and address reliability gaps

  • Ensure system reliability and performance

Collaboration

  • Work effectively across multiple teams

  • Build strong relationships with stakeholders

  • Drive reliability alignment between teams

  • Facilitate reliability discussions and decisions

  • Share knowledge and best practices

Mentorship

  • Mentor reliability leaders across teams

  • Guide reliability decisions

  • Share SRE expertise

  • Develop reliability talent

  • Build reliability leadership capabilities

Expectations

Technical Excellence

  • Deep expertise in multiple SRE areas

  • Strong system design skills

  • Ability to make complex reliability decisions

  • Understanding of cross-team reliability challenges

  • Knowledge of industry best practices

Leadership

  • Ability to lead cross-team initiatives

  • Strong reliability decision-making skills

  • Effective communication across teams

  • Strategic thinking and planning

  • Mentorship and coaching abilities

Impact

  • Drive SRE excellence across teams

  • Improve reliability strategy and design

  • Enhance team productivity and effectiveness

  • Foster reliability innovation

  • Build reliability capabilities

Career Progression

Path to IC5

  • Demonstrate department-wide reliability leadership

  • Drive strategic reliability initiatives

  • Show significant impact across the department

  • Mentor and develop reliability leaders

  • Contribute to department success

Path to Management

  • Develop strong leadership skills

  • Build cross-team relationships

  • Drive reliability strategy

  • Mentor and develop others

  • Show potential for people management
