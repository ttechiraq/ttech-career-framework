IC6 Site Reliability Engineer

Overview

IC6 Site Reliability Engineers are principal-level technical leaders who shape company-wide reliability direction and influence reliability standards across the company. They have significant impact across the entire company and are responsible for reliability vision development and strategic reliability direction.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Leadership

  • Shape company-wide reliability direction

  • Drive strategic reliability initiatives

  • Make company-wide reliability decisions

  • Mentor company reliability leaders

  • Define reliability vision

System Design

  • Design company-wide reliability strategies

  • Create reliability standards and best practices

  • Drive reliability strategy

  • Address company-wide reliability challenges

  • Ensure system reliability and performance

Collaboration

  • Work effectively across the company

  • Build strong relationships with stakeholders

  • Drive reliability alignment

  • Facilitate reliability discussions

  • Share knowledge and best practices

Mentorship

  • Mentor company reliability leaders

  • Guide reliability decisions

  • Share SRE expertise

  • Develop reliability talent

  • Build reliability leadership capabilities

Expectations

Technical Excellence

  • Expert knowledge across SRE areas

  • Mastery of system design

  • Ability to make company-wide reliability decisions

  • Understanding of company-wide reliability challenges

  • Comprehensive knowledge of industry best practices

Leadership

  • Ability to lead company-wide initiatives

  • Strong strategic decision-making skills

  • Effective communication across the company

  • Strategic thinking and planning

  • Mentorship and coaching abilities

Impact

  • Drive SRE excellence across the company

  • Improve reliability strategy and design

  • Enhance company productivity and effectiveness

  • Foster reliability innovation

  • Build reliability capabilities

Career Progression

Path to IC7

  • Demonstrate senior company-wide reliability leadership

  • Drive strategic reliability initiatives

  • Show significant impact across the company

  • Mentor and develop reliability leaders

  • Contribute to company success

Path to Management

  • Develop strong leadership skills

  • Build company-wide relationships

  • Drive reliability strategy

  • Mentor and develop others

  • Show potential for people management
