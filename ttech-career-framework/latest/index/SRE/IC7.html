IC7 Site Reliability Engineer

Overview

IC7 Site Reliability Engineers are senior principal-level technical leaders who drive company-wide reliability excellence and innovation. They have the highest level of impact across the entire company and are responsible for setting the strategic direction for reliability engineering and driving technical excellence.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Leadership

  • Drive company-wide reliability excellence

  • Set strategic reliability direction

  • Make critical company-wide reliability decisions

  • Mentor senior reliability leaders

  • Define long-term reliability vision

System Design

  • Design innovative reliability strategies

  • Establish company-wide reliability standards

  • Drive reliability innovation

  • Solve complex company-wide reliability challenges

  • Ensure world-class system reliability

Collaboration

  • Lead company-wide reliability initiatives

  • Build strategic relationships with stakeholders

  • Drive reliability transformation

  • Lead reliability discussions

  • Share expertise and best practices

Mentorship

  • Mentor senior reliability leaders

  • Guide strategic reliability decisions

  • Share deep SRE expertise

  • Develop future reliability leaders

  • Build world-class reliability capabilities

Expectations

Technical Excellence

  • Mastery across all SRE domains

  • Expert system design capabilities

  • Ability to make critical company-wide decisions

  • Deep understanding of reliability challenges

  • Industry-leading knowledge and expertise

Leadership

  • Exceptional company-wide leadership

  • Outstanding strategic decision-making

  • Masterful communication skills

  • Visionary thinking and planning

  • Expert mentorship and coaching

Impact

  • Drive company-wide reliability excellence

  • Transform reliability strategy and design

  • Maximize company productivity and effectiveness

  • Lead reliability innovation

  • Build world-class reliability capabilities

Career Progression

Continued Growth

  • Continue to drive reliability excellence

  • Mentor and develop senior leaders

  • Contribute to company success

  • Lead innovation initiatives

  • Build future capabilities

Path to Management

  • Develop executive leadership skills

  • Build strategic relationships

  • Drive company-wide strategy

  • Mentor senior leaders

  • Show potential for executive management
