IC3 Site Reliability Engineer

Overview

IC3 Site Reliability Engineers are senior-level professionals who provide technical leadership in system reliability and architecture. They handle complex distributed systems and drive organization-wide reliability standards and practices.

Key Responsibilities

Area Responsibilities

System Reliability

  • Define and drive reliability strategy

  • Design comprehensive monitoring and observability

  • Establish organization-wide SLOs and SLIs

  • Lead major incident response

  • Ensure system resilience

Infrastructure

  • Design and optimize cloud architecture

  • Drive Infrastructure as Code standards

  • Optimize resource efficiency

  • Set security standards

  • Define deployment strategies

Automation

  • Design and implement automation frameworks

  • Build advanced deployment systems

  • Create self-healing architectures

  • Drive chaos engineering programs

  • Optimize operational workflows

Performance

  • Design and optimize system architecture

  • Resolve complex performance issues

  • Implement advanced optimizations

  • Drive capacity planning strategy

  • Design comprehensive testing strategies

Expectations

Area Expectations

Technical Skills

  • Expert knowledge of cloud platforms

  • Mastery of monitoring and observability

  • Advanced automation and tooling

  • Expert CI/CD implementation

  • Deep system architecture knowledge

Collaboration

  • Take full ownership of cross-team initiatives and their success

  • Mentor senior SREs and take responsibility for their development

  • Drive organizational improvements with clear ownership of outcomes

  • Set and maintain technical standards with clear rationale

  • Share expertise across teams proactively

  • Take responsibility for organization-wide reliability

Impact

  • Take full ownership of organization-wide reliability initiatives

  • Lead major incident response with clear ownership of outcomes

  • Set and maintain best practices with comprehensive documentation

  • Take responsibility for system architecture improvements

  • Proactively identify and address systemic reliability risks

  • Ensure long-term sustainability of solutions

  • Take ownership of business impact of reliability decisions

  • Drive innovation in reliability practices

Growth Path

As an IC3 SRE, focus on: * Taking full ownership of organization-wide reliability initiatives * Mentoring and developing other SREs with clear responsibility * Setting technical direction with long-term vision * Influencing business decisions with clear ownership * Contributing to industry best practices * Taking initiative in identifying and solving systemic issues * Ensuring comprehensive documentation and knowledge transfer * Taking ownership of long-term system reliability and scalability * Driving innovation in reliability practices and tools
