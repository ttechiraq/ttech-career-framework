IC5 Site Reliability Engineer

Overview

IC5 Site Reliability Engineers are senior staff-level technical leaders who drive department-wide reliability strategy and lead complex reliability initiatives. They have significant impact across their department and are responsible for strategic reliability decisions and reliability strategy development.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Leadership

  • Drive department-wide reliability strategy

  • Lead complex reliability initiatives

  • Make strategic reliability decisions

  • Mentor department reliability leaders

  • Shape reliability direction

System Design

  • Design department-wide reliability strategies

  • Create reliability standards and best practices

  • Drive reliability strategy

  • Address complex reliability challenges

  • Ensure system reliability and performance

Collaboration

  • Work effectively across the department

  • Build strong relationships with stakeholders

  • Drive reliability alignment

  • Facilitate reliability discussions

  • Share knowledge and best practices

Mentorship

  • Mentor department reliability leaders

  • Guide reliability decisions

  • Share SRE expertise

  • Develop reliability talent

  • Build reliability leadership capabilities

Expectations

Technical Excellence

  • Expert knowledge in multiple SRE areas

  • Advanced system design skills

  • Ability to make strategic reliability decisions

  • Understanding of department-wide reliability challenges

  • Deep knowledge of industry best practices

Leadership

  • Ability to lead department-wide initiatives

  • Strong strategic decision-making skills

  • Effective communication across the department

  • Strategic thinking and planning

  • Mentorship and coaching abilities

Impact

  • Drive SRE excellence across the department

  • Improve reliability strategy and design

  • Enhance department productivity and effectiveness

  • Foster reliability innovation

  • Build reliability capabilities

Career Progression

Path to IC6

  • Demonstrate company-wide reliability leadership

  • Drive strategic reliability initiatives

  • Show significant impact across the company

  • Mentor and develop reliability leaders

  • Contribute to company success

Path to Management

  • Develop strong leadership skills

  • Build department-wide relationships

  • Drive reliability strategy

  • Mentor and develop others

  • Show potential for people management
