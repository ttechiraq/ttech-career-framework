IC2 Site Reliability Engineer

Overview

IC2 Site Reliability Engineers are mid-level professionals who work independently on reliability improvements and lead incident response. They handle multiple services and systems, ensuring their reliability and performance across teams.

Key Responsibilities

Area Responsibilities

System Reliability

  • Design and implement monitoring and alerting

  • Set up comprehensive logging and tracing

  • Define and maintain SLOs and SLIs

  • Lead incident response

  • Ensure system availability

Infrastructure

  • Manage cloud infrastructure independently

  • Implement Infrastructure as Code

  • Optimize resource usage

  • Ensure security compliance

  • Manage deployments

Automation

  • Write complex automation scripts

  • Build and maintain deployment pipelines

  • Implement self-healing systems

  • Lead chaos engineering initiatives

  • Optimize workflows

Performance

  • Analyze and optimize system performance

  • Identify and resolve bottlenecks

  • Implement complex optimizations

  • Lead capacity planning

  • Design and execute load tests

Expectations

Area Expectations

Technical Skills

  • Deep understanding of cloud platforms

  • Expertise in monitoring and observability

  • Advanced scripting and automation

  • Strong CI/CD implementation skills

  • Advanced troubleshooting and debugging

Collaboration

  • Work independently and take full ownership of initiatives

  • Mentor junior SREs and take responsibility for their growth

  • Collaborate across teams and take ownership of cross-team solutions

  • Drive process improvements with clear ownership of outcomes

  • Share knowledge and best practices proactively

  • Take responsibility for team success and reliability

Impact

  • Take full ownership of reliability improvements and their success

  • Lead incident response with clear ownership of resolution

  • Implement and maintain best practices with clear documentation

  • Take responsibility for system architecture improvements

  • Proactively identify and address reliability risks

  • Ensure long-term sustainability of solutions

  • Take ownership of system performance and availability

Growth Path

To progress to IC3, IC2 SREs should focus on: * Taking full ownership of complex reliability initiatives * Mentoring senior team members and developing leadership * Driving architectural improvements with clear ownership * Setting and maintaining reliability standards * Influencing organization-wide practices * Taking initiative in identifying and solving systemic issues * Ensuring proper documentation and knowledge transfer * Taking ownership of long-term system reliability
